Engineer Turned Educator

Welcome to my corner of the digital world, where education and technology intersect in exciting and meaningful ways. With a background in Computer Science Engineering and seven years of experience as a dedicated Tech Educator, I've had the privilege of leading over 8,000 coding sessions globally, inspiring learners of all ages to embrace the world of technology.

My journey from the engineering field to the classroom has been driven by a passion for making complex ideas accessible and engaging. I believe that education should be as dynamic as the world we live in, and I strive to create learning experiences that are both informative and enjoyable. Whether it's breaking down the intricacies of coding or guiding students through the maze of tech, my goal is to ignite curiosity and foster a love for learning. 

When I'm not shaping the minds of tomorrow, you'll find me immersed in creative projects that blend technology with innovation. I’m constantly exploring new ways to bring energy and relevance to education, ensuring that every session I lead is not just a lesson, but an experience.

So whether you're here to learn more about my work, explore potential collaborations, or simply get to know me better, I'm glad you're here. Let's embark on this journey of discovery together 🚀

Curious Minds Ask

What’s your teaching philosophy?

I believe that learners excel when they can dive in, experiment, and explore. 🌱 Every learner is unique, with their own learning style and personality, which is why I tailor my approach to meet their individual needs. Creativity and adaptability are at the heart of my teaching philosophy. 🎨✨

How do you engage students?

I engage students by turning learning into an adventure where curiosity is the compass and fun is the destination. By customizing my approach to each student’s unique style, I ensure they stay as motivated as a kid at an amusement park 🎢.
With the help of interactive tools and a sprinkle of technology magic ✨, I keep lessons exciting and lively. I create a classroom culture where questions are welcomed, mistakes are just stepping stones, and positive reinforcement is as common as high-fives 🖐🏻.
Together, we turn learning into a fun-filled quest where every student gets to be the hero of their own story, celebrating each milestone along the way🛣️.

 Learning should be fun🥳, not a snooze-fest 😴, Right ?

What subjects do you teach?

I specialize in Python, Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and C#, and well-versed in frameworks like Bootstrap, React Native, A-Frame, and Flask. My expertise spans Web Development, Android App Development, Game Design, Blockchain, 3D Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, AI, ML, Data Visualization, and Data Science.
I have worked on Self-Driving Car Simulations, Chatbots, Cloud Computing, Version Control, Software Engineering, Networking, and Web Scraping projects.
